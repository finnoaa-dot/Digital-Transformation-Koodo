# Digital Transformation Elevates Customer Experience

A comprehensive case study on transforming customer support for Koodo, a leading prepaid mobile provider, through strategic research, design, and engineering.

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how digital transformation can simultaneously improve customer experience and reduce operational costs. By leveraging user research and design thinking, we developed innovative solutions that addressed the needs of Koodo's tech-savvy prepaid mobile customer base.

## Methodology

- **Competitive Analysis** – Benchmarking against industry leaders
- **Opt-In Panel Survey** – Quantitative insights from target audience
- **Moderated In-Depth Interviews** – Qualitative user discovery
- **Feature Desirability Survey** – Validation of proposed features
- **Experience Audit** – Comprehensive evaluation of current state

## Key Technologies

- Figma (Design & Prototyping)
- Lookback (User Research)
- Qualtrics (Survey Platform)
- UserInterviews (Recruitment)

## Business Impact

- **50% reduction** in call volume
- **15% increase** in customer containment rate
- **6 weeks** of strategy, research, and engineering

## Deliverables

- In-depth user research findings identifying end-user pain points
- Feature prioritization quantitative survey results
- Recommendation to develop **Koodo Assist**, an innovative support solution
- Strategic roadmap for implementation

## Key Learnings

Through in-depth interviews with customers, we uncovered critical pain points in the current support experience. These findings directly informed the design and development strategy for Koodo Assist, ensuring product-market fit and user adoption.
