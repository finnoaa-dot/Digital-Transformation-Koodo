# Digital Transformation Elevates Customer Experience

A comprehensive case study on transforming customer support for Koodo, a leading prepaid mobile provider, through strategic research, design, and engineering.

## Project Overview

My work on this project demonstrates how digital transformation can simultaneously improve customer experience and reduce operational costs. By leveraging user research and design thinking, we developed innovative solutions that streamlined support operations and enhanced user satisfaction.

## Methodology

- **Competitive Analysis** – Benchmarking against industry leaders
- **Opt-In Panel Survey** – Quantitative insights from target audience
- **Moderated In-Depth Interviews** – Qualitative user discovery
- **Feature Desirability Survey** – Validation of proposed features
- **Experience Audit** – Comprehensive evaluation of current state

### Research Process
I utilized “mixed methods” of qualitative and quantitative research approach, including an opt-in current customer poll, in-depth qualitative interviews with former, current and non-customers, and a comprehensive feature desirability survey, the insights from which were folded into a prioritization framework and digital ecosystem roadmap.


## Key Technologies

- Figma (Design & Prototyping)

## User Research Findings

Our research uncovered critical pain points in the current support experience, directly informing the design and development strategy.

### Research Statements & Goals 
Understand customer needs and pain points with the current Koodo experience.      
Discover user/former user needs and pain points with their current Koodo experience.      
Understand what they expect and want out of an app or digital solution.      
Understand what tasks consumers want to complete via a website, an app, in-person, or over the phone, and why they prefer the method.      
What motivates them to come back to an app/solution?  

### Research Methodology
Qualitative Survey: In-depth interviews with six current and former Koodo customers to understand their experiences, needs, and pain points.      
Opt-In Survey: An online survey distributed to 624 current Koodo customers to gather insights on their interest in a Koodo app and their preferred features.      
App Feature Desirability Survey: An online survey of 173 Canadian mobile service provider app consumers to understand their most desired app features and their satisfaction with existing features.   
Competitive Analysis: Analyze competitor apps to identify industry best practices and opportunities for differentiation.
Current Experience Audit: Evaluate the current Koodo website and customer experience to identify areas for improvement.

## Business Impact

- **50% reduction** in call volume
- **15% increase** in customer containment rate
- **6 weeks** of strategy, research, and engineering

## Deliverables

- In-depth user research findings identifying end-user pain points
- Feature prioritization quantitative survey results
- Recommendation to develop **Koodo Assist**, an innovative async chat support solution
- Strategic roadmap for implementation

### Key Deliverables Overview

Final Client Presentation: A comprehensive presentation deck outlining the research findings, strategic recommendations, and proposed features for the Koodo mobile app.      
Concept Designs: Visual mockups of the proposed Koodo mobile app, showcasing the user interface and key features.      
Technical Proof of Concepts: Demonstrations of the technical feasibility of key features, such as authentication and eSIM activation.      
Engineering Roadmap: A roadmap outlining the technical development plan for the Koodo mobile app.


## Key Learnings

This project allowed me to execute a robust research plan which included both qual and quant exercises, in order to create a complete digital transformation for this client. 
Specifically, this effort reinforced the value of a multi-channel customer experience, as our participant customers expressed a desire to interact with Koodo through various channels, including web, app, SMS, and email.
